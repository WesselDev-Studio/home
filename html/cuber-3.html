Cuber 3: Schmidt

About The Game

Cuber 3: Schmidt is a sequel of Cuber 2: Ice Age. Cuber 3: Schmidt is a 2D platformer game with enemies shooting at the player and with ice but better.

The main goal is to get to the end of levels and jump into the portal. Side goals are killing the enemies via bombs and collecting coins to score points.
